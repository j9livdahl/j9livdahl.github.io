Who We Are

Non-Profit Yoga Studio

One Yoga first opened its doors in September 2002. When the original owner decided she no longer wanted to run the studio, the teachers banded together to keep it running. In 2005, they decided to make One Yoga a nonprofit studio with the mission of making yoga accessible to all. We received our official 501(c)(3) status in 2007 and the studio and the mission have both continued to grow. What we once called our Outreach Program was renamed Karma Tribe. Hours

One Yoga is open at least 15 minutes prior to class. We also have a staff member at the front desk Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. until 1:00 p.m and 3:45 p.m. until 7:45 p.m.

Studio Space

Our studio is a welcoming place where you can relax, quiet your mind, and be fully present. It's a warm and inviting place that facilitates your inward, centering journey. In our lounge, you will always find free delicious tea, filtered water, and a collection of reading materials on yoga and wellness to inspire you. We have several changing rooms for your convenience.
